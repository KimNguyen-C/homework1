Welcome to bōba!

At bōba, we believe bubble tea is more than just a drink – it’s an experience. Every cup is crafted with care, blending the art of traditional tea brewing with a modern twist of fun, creativity, and bold flavors. From creamy milk teas to refreshing fruit blends, sparkling lattes to seasonal specialties, there’s always something new to sip and savor.

What makes us different? We focus on quality and customization. Our teas are brewed fresh daily, our ingredients are carefully selected, and our toppings – from classic tapioca pearls to fruit jellies, popping boba, and silky puddings – are made to let you create your perfect drink. Whether you like it sweet, less sweet, icy cold, or warm and cozy, every sip is just the way you like it.

But we’re more than just drinks. bōba is designed to be a space where you can slow down, connect, and enjoy life’s little moments. Whether you’re meeting friends, grabbing a study break, or simply treating yourself after a long day, our store is your go-to spot for comfort, community, and joy.

Freshly brewed. Fully customizable. Always delicious. Come on in, sip your favorite blend, and discover why bubble tea is best shared with the people you love.
